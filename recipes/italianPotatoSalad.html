Vintage Lemonade

In the 1800s this how people made lemonade-You can do it too! It's not that difficult, and tastes wonderful!

Prep Time Cook Time Total Time Servings Yield
45 Min 20 Min 1 hr 5 mins 4 4 Servings

Ingredients

  • 5 lemons
  • 1 1/4 cups white sugar
  • 1 1/4 quarts water

Directions

  • Step 1

    • Peel the rinds from the 5 lemons and cut them into 1/2 inch slices. Set the lemons aside.

  • Step 2

    • Place the rinds in a bowl and sprinkle the sugar over them. Let this stand for about one hour, so that the sugar begins to soak up the oils from the lemons.

  • Step 3

    • Squeeze the lemons in a covered saucepan and then pour hot water over the sugared lemon rinds. Allow this mixture to cool for 20 minutes and then remove the rinds.

  • Step 4

    • Squeeze the lemons into anotehr bowl. Pour the juice through a strainer into the sugar mixture. Stir well, pour into pitcher and pop it in the fridge! Serve with ice cubes.
